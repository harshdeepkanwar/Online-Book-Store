Shipping Policies:

If a customer choose, normal delivery, than the shipment will be done by either Indian Post Registered parcel or by normal courier companies which take 6-10 days to deliver an Order

If a customer chooses, fast delivery, than the shipment will be done by either Speed post or by some good pvt. Courier companies. It will take 3-6 days to deliver a parcel

If a Customer is from jaipur and ready to collect from our office (Patel marg, mansroavar, jaipur) than there will be no shipping charge. Parcel will be hand over in office within 3 days of order

Refund Policies:

Although we take full care, while sending any books to minimum the chances of mis-shipment. Below are our terms and conditions for refund Process

If a customer wants to cancel a order before shipment, there will be a 5% deduction of order amount and remaining amount will be refunded back to customer account.

If a customer want to cancel an order after shipment or want to return after delivery due to any reason, both side shipment cost be beared by the customer, we will process the refund only after receipt of books in our jaipur office

For the refund purpose, a customer needs to submit bank details in which he want to have refund. Bank details include Account holder Name, A/c no, IFSC code and Branch Name. All this details need to submit online to our email ID @gmail.com

It will take 3-6 days to reflect amount in your bank account.
